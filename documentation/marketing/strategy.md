# Marketing Strategy: Building Authority-First Japanese Learning Gateway

## Executive Summary

Based on current metrics (45k+ monthly impressions, 180+ clicks/week across 47+ countries), we're implementing an authority-first marketing strategy that positions MichiKanji as the **definitive opinionated guide for kanji learning** rather than a passive dictionary. Recent search data reveals massive opportunities in individual kanji optimization, stroke order content, and geographic market expansion. This approach prioritizes long-term trust and influence over immediate revenue, creating a sustainable foundation for high-value monetization.

## Strategic Foundation

### Market Position: "The Kanji Learning Authority"

**Not**: Generic kanji dictionary
**Yes**: Opinionated learning guide with expert curation

**Brand Promise**: "We don't just show you kanji—we guide you to master them the right way."

### Target Audience Segments

#### Primary: Serious Self-Learners
- **Profile**: Adults learning Japanese independently
- **Pain Points**: Information overload, lack of structure, unclear progress
- **Value**: Clear learning paths, expert guidance, progress tracking

#### Secondary: Teachers & Tutors  
- **Profile**: Japanese language educators
- **Pain Points**: Resource preparation time, student engagement, progress tracking
- **Value**: Ready-made materials, student progress insights, classroom tools

#### Tertiary: Students & Hobby Learners
- **Profile**: Casual learners, anime fans, culture enthusiasts  
- **Pain Points**: Motivation, complexity, intimidation
- **Value**: Approachable introduction, gamification, cultural context
